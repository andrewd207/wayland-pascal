THIRD-PARTY NOTICES
===================

This project's Wayland protocol bindings are generated (by wayland-gen) from the
upstream Wayland protocol descriptions:

  - the core protocol:   wayland.xml            (from the `wayland` project)
  - extension protocols: wayland-protocols/     (stable, unstable, staging)

The generated Pascal units — wayland-rt/.../wayland.pas and every
wayland-{stable,unstable,staging}/.../*_protocol.pas — are derived works of those
XML descriptions. The descriptions are licensed under the MIT (Expat) license by
their respective authors; the generated bindings are therefore distributed in
accordance with that license, in addition to this project's own BSD-3-Clause
license (see LICENSE) which covers the generator and the runtime.

protocol XML. The authoritative notice for each protocol is the <copyright>
